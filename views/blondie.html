Debbie Harry

Singer, Songwriter, Actress


Blondie - "Heart of Glass"

Deborah Ann "Debbie" Harry (born July 1, 1945) is an American singer-songwriter and actress, best known as the lead singer of the new wave and punk rock band Blondie. She recorded several worldwide number one singles with Blondie during the 1970s and 1980s.

My Top 5 Favorite Debbie Harry Songs

  • 1978 - Heart of Glass - No.1 in the UK and was the second highest selling single of 1979.

  • 1979 - Atomic - The song was produced as a mixture of new wave, rock and disco.

  • 1980 - Call Me - "Call Me" was number one for six consecutive weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

  • 1980 - The Tide Is High - Originally produced by Duke Reid and performed by the Jamaican group the Paragons, with John Holt as lead singer.

  • 1980 - Rapture - "Rapture" is a combination of disco, funk, and hip hop.
