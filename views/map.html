About us

Jeremy Lewis, CEO

Jeremy Lewis worked at the start-up Everwise. There he managed customer feedback, proposing product improvements and configuring the application to meet client needs. His duties included quality assurance of the platform in preparation for releases of software updates to ensure data integrity and a seamless customer experience. Before working at Everwise, Jeremy worked at MediaOcean as a Support Engineer and at UBS as a Client Resolution Specialist.

Nikhil Sinha, CTO

Nikhil Sinha is a software developer with over ten years of experience. He has worked in both the financial sector and legal technology sector. As a Lead Software Developer and Architect at Omnivere, he is in charge of the Direct E-Discovery Platform, including the front-end website and the search systems behind it. He has an interest in computer graphics and is currently learning OpenCV, an image and video manipulation software library.

Prior to working at Omnivere, Nick worked at a variety of financial firms, including Thomson-Reuters, Credit-Suisse, and Susquehanna International Group. Most of this was spent building distributed risk software. Before starting in the financial sector, he spent a summer at Electronic Arts, working on the video game adaptation of James Bond: From Russia with Love.

About LandNav Planner

Travel through uncertain terrain conditions risks delays and failure of reaching one's objective.
